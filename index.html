World Red Carpet Premiere

November 2, 2025 | Bella Concert Hall, Calgary

We are thrilled to announce that FOREIGN HOMELAND will make its World Red Carpet Premiere at Bella Concert Hall. This marks the first-ever Iranian red-carpet movie premiere in Calgary, a milestone not just for our team, but also for the Persian community and Alberta's growing independent film scene.
