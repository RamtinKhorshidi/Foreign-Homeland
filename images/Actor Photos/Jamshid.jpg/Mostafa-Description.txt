Mostafa plays Jamshid, the father, in Foreign Homeland, marking his debut in a feature film. Mostafa is a Calgary-based actor and engineer with a lifelong love for theatre and film. Alongside earning two MSc degrees and building a career in engineering, he has acted in theatre, appeared in the short film The Taste of Brazilian Coffee, and narrated various projects. Since immigrating to Canada in 2011, he has continued creating and performing in his spare time.
