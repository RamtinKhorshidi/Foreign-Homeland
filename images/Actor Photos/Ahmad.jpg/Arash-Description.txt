Arash plays Ahmad, the youngest brother, in Foreign Homeland. He is a Calgary-based interdisciplinary artist of Indo-Persian heritage, working across theatre, dance, and movement storytelling. He is currently completing a Master's degree in Interdisciplinary Creation and Research at the University of Calgary, focusing on themes of identity and selfhood among first-generation Indo-Persian immigrants. With over 15 years of experience, Arash has worked as a playwright, actor, dancer, and poet. His work blends personal narrative with cultural exploration, creating performances that connect tradition with contemporary expression.
